Exchange & Return

Our Policy Roots will gladly exchange or refund qualified items purchased up to 30 days after the purchase date with an original receipt. Items must be in their original state: unwashed, unworn, unaltered and must have all tickets attached. Not applicable on final sale items. FINAL SALE MERCHANDISE - No refunds, credits or exchanges on the following: Any items ending in $0.87 or $0.98, any sale item included in an additional percentage off promotion, custom or personalized orders, gift cards, jewellery, swimwear, limited edition, masks or charitable item. Online purchases made on Roots.com or Shipped from Store merchandise may be returned by mail with an original receipt within 30 days after receiving the package. We are unable to process exchanges by mail at this time. Should you wish to return your items to a Roots store location, you may do so within 30 days of reopening. All returned merchandise will be refunded in the original form of payment. Returned merchandise that was originally paid for by alternate tenders (i.e Gift Certificates etc.) other than cash, debit or credit card will receive an exchange or refund in the form of a store credit onto a Roots Gift Card. Cash will not be issued. All returns and price adjustments are subject to validation and approval at Roots' discretion. To protect our customers from fraudulent activity, Roots reserves the right to request valid photo ID for the purposes of validating customer information when processing a return or exchange. For more information, please contact our Customer Service Team locally at 647-799-2694 or toll free at 1-888-30-ROOTS (76687).
